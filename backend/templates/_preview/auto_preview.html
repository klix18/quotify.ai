Liability Limits
These limits define the maximum your policy will pay for injuries or damage you cause to others.
Bodily Injury $100,000 / $300,000
Covers medical expenses and legal costs if you injure someone in an accident you caused.
Property Damage $100,000
Pays to repair or replace another person's property that you damage in an at-fault accident.
Medical Payments $5,000
Covers medical expenses for you and your passengers regardless of who is at fault.
UM/UIM Bodily Injury $100,000 / $300,000
